About PulseQuant

What is PulseQuant?

PulseQuant is a free, professional-grade stock charting platform built specifically for the Indian market. We provide clean, fast, and reliable charts powered by TradingView technology, allowing traders and investors to analyze over 7,000 Indian stocks without any registration or hidden costs.

Our platform features multiple watchlist management, smart search with auto-complete, and quick navigation between stocks using Previous/Next buttons or keyboard shortcuts. We believe that powerful market analysis tools should be accessible to everyone, which is why we keep everything free and store your data locally in your browser for complete privacy.

With PulseQuant, you get line charts by default for faster loading, with the option to switch to detailed candlestick patterns when you need deeper analysis. All charts show 1-year data by default, giving you the perfect balance of historical context and performance. Whether you're tracking the NIFTY 50, building sector-specific watchlists, or analyzing mid-cap stocks, PulseQuant gives you the tools you need to make informed trading decisions.

How PulseQuant Works

1

Create Watchlists

Build multiple watchlists with up to 500 stocks each. Import via CSV or search from our database of 7,000+ Indian stocks.

2

View Charts

Click any stock to open TradingView charts with 1-year default view. Switch between line and candlestick with one click.

3

Navigate Easily

Use Previous/Next buttons to move through your watchlist. Keyboard shortcuts (← → arrows) are also supported.

4

Privacy First

Your watchlists stay in your browser. No accounts, no cloud storage, complete privacy. Create a profile only if you want to save your data.
